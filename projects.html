Kalaripayattu Training & Kalari Centre Facilities

Kalaripayattu, the ancient martial art of Kerala, trains the body and mind through strength, agility, and discipline. Training begins with warm-ups, body conditioning, and vadivu (animal postures), followed by strikes, kicks, and defensive techniques. Advanced training includes weapon practice with sticks, swords, and shields, along with meditation and breathing exercises to develop focus and inner balance.

Our Kalari centre is designed to provide a traditional yet comfortable training environment. The facility includes a well-prepared kalari training ground with sacred setup, spacious practice areas, and safe flooring for intense movements. Separate sections are available for weapon training and physical conditioning. The centre also offers guidance in kalari marma chikitsa (traditional healing and massage), rest areas for students, and modern amenities to ensure safety and comfort.

By combining time-honored and authentic training methods with carefully maintained facilities, a supportive learning environment, and experienced instructors, the Kalari centre provides an ideal atmosphere for students of all ages to immerse themselves in, practice diligently, and help preserve the rich cultural heritage and profound traditions of this ancient martial art, fostering not only physical strength and skill but also discipline, focus, and a deep appreciation for its historical significance.
For the past 14 years, Kashi Gurukkal has been a vibrant presence in the soil of Onattukara, actively engaged in Kalari, martial arts, Marma therapy, traditional medicine, and agriculture. Beyond barriers of caste and religion, he has nurtured a large community of disciples. Many outstanding practitioners, who have proven their skills within Kerala, across India, and even abroad, are the contributions of the Gurukkal. Kalari Marma Treatment and Facilities

This therapy combines ancient wisdom with natural techniques such as herbal oils, therapeutic massages, and pressure-point activation to relieve pain, enhance circulation, and restore balance in the body. It is highly effective for sports injuries, joint pain, stress relief, and overall rejuvenation.
