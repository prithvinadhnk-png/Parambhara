Beyond spirituality and healing, the foundation has also become a center for service and volunteer initiatives. With its focus on compassion, social responsibility, and cultural preservation, Parambhara has taken up numerous service projects that benefit the community at large. In a remarkably short span of time, the foundation has grown into a model institution, inspiring many with its dedication to tradition, humanity, and spiritual growth.

Parambhara Foundation continues its journey with the vision of creating a harmonious society where culture, health, spirituality, and service come together for the well-being of all.

Upcoming Mega Event

Maha Varaha Dhanvanthari Yagam

In the current era of Kaliyuga, karma holds profound significance. Virtuous actions not only elevate the individual but also bring blessings to future generations. Among the most effective means to purify karma are yagas (sacrificial rituals), which confer prosperity, health, longevity, wealth, and mental clarity.

This sacred ritual will be conducted over three days, invoking divine blessings for the welfare, prosperity, and peace of all beings.
